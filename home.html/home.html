Kew College Suzhou Industrial Park was established in 2012 and moved to a new purpose-built campus in 2015, sharing the campus with
its sister school kew College Suzhou. Dulwich College Suzhou Industrial Park is a co-educational boarding school for Chinese and international
students aged 14 to 19, approximately 60% of our students choose to board.
The school is academically top-notch and offers a three- or four-year international programme, culminating in the International General Certificate of Secondary
Education (IGCSE) and A-Level examinations.

Admission Requirements

All applicants must pass an entrance examination conducted by the school.
They are assessed for adaptability to the International Curriculum in English and Mathematics.
